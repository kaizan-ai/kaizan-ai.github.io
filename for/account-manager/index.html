Objections, answered

The questions client service / account managers actually ask us.

Will the AI sound like me?
It learns your voice from your last 90 days of email. Drafts come back in your tone. Editable in one click.
What if it sends something wrong?
It doesn’t send anything until you hit go. Optional auto-send only for low-stakes templates you pre-approve.
Will my clients know an AI is involved?
You decide. Most AMs don’t disclose draft assistance any more than they disclose Grammarly. Invisible to the client unless you choose otherwise.
How long to ramp?
Day one: morning briefing. Week two: drafts in your voice. Week four: the six-hour-a-week saving most AMs report.
